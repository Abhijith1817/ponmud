PONMUDI HILL TOP

One of the best way to spend time while visiting the city. You can enjoy a long drive up the winding hills for a view in the golden peak of the city. And there are almost 22 hair pin bends. You can stop at anywhere you think it's safe and enjoy the view. The hill top is really a great experience. On the way you can also stop at kallar where you can get down in the water and enjoy the view.Don't ever miss the meenmutty waterfalls on the way back from the hill top which is another great tourist spot. Ticket rate : 40 rs per head Time : Opens at 8 30 Directions : Peroorkada – Nedumangad- Chullimanoor – Vithura – Ponmudi.
